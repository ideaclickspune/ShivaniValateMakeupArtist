Know Me

Meet Shivani Valate

Shivani Valate is a professional makeup artist known for her expertise in bridal makeovers and all types of makeup styling from traditional and HD makeup to party, engagement, and photoshoot looks. With her creative touch and attention to detail, she enhances every client’s natural beauty, ensuring a flawless and long-lasting finish.

She believes that makeup is not just about transformation, but about confidence and self-expression. With years of hands-on experience and passion for beauty, she tailors every look according to her client’s features, occasion, and personal style.

From Bridal and Engagement Makeup to Party, Editorial, and Glam Looks, Shivani delivers professional artistry with perfection. Her goal is simple: “To make every woman feel confident, radiant, and beautiful.”
